Types of deer

There are technically 2 kinds of deer
Antlerless
Antlered
Of these two kinds, there exists upwards of 80 different subspecies!

Some of these species include:

  • White Tail
  • Mule
  • Blacktail
  • Caribou
  • Moose
  • Reindeer
The countries with the highest density of roe deer are as follows
  1. Germany with 3,000,000
  2. France with 1,200,000
  3. Austria with 750,000
